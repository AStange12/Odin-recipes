Protien Oats

Description

This is my protien oatmeal recipe, it has 300 calories and 33g of protien and only takes a couple minuets.

Ingredients

  • Quick Oats
  • Protien Powder
  • 0-sugar Maple Syrup
  • Unsweetend Almond Milk
  • Ground Cinnamon
  • Vinilla Extract
  • Stevia
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Add 1/2 cup each of oats and milk to bowl
  2. Add tbsp of cinnamon, 1/2 tbsp each of vinilla and stevia, and tsp of salt to bowl
  3. Mix till combined and put in microwave for 1.5 minuets
  4. Once done in microwave, add scoop of protien powder and mix, drizzleing in syrup while mixing to help combine
  5. Finally, dust top with cinnamon, drizzle some syrup and add dash of salt
